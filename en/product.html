1. Easy & Safe usage

Lumos Candle uses soy wax and a wood wick.
Experience the world's first auto-ignition candle 'Lumos Candle'.


2. NO Paraffin

Lumos Candle does not use paraffin wax.
We use only premium US soy wax.


3. Easy charging type

You don't need to buy a gas lighter or a new ignition tool anymore.
Since the battery is built into the pad, you can easily charge it using a USB charger.
When fully charged once, it can be used for more than 4 months.


4. Easy refill replacement

If you used up all the refill candles or wanted a new scent, simply replace the refill candle right away.
Enjoy a colorful candle life with more than 7 different scents.


5. Safe Design

Lumos Candle is designed to minimize shaking.
The refill candle is securely attached to the pad and is safe for general shaking.
