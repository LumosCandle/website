Lumos Candle FAQ

Q. What is Lumos Candle?
Lumos Candle is a magical candle that lights up with just a touch of a button.
Q. How is it different from traditional candles?
Traditional candles require lighters or matches, which can be inconvenient and risky.

Lumos Candle eliminates this hassle. Simply press the button on the pad to ignite the wick instantly. It is perfect for those who find lighters difficult or dangerous to use.

Also, unlike disposable lighters, the Lumos Candle pad is rechargeable and semi-permanent.
Q. How does it work?
It uses electric arc discharge to ignite the wick.
Place the refill candle on the Lumos pad, press the button, and the wick ignites instantly.

Refill Candle FAQ

Q. Can I use regular candles?
No, you must use Lumos dedicated refill candles. They are designed with special wicks for auto-ignition.
Q. How long does a refill candle last?
It lasts for about 40 hours. (Duration may vary depending on usage environment.)
Q. How do I replace the refill candle?
It is very easy to attach and detach.
Simply remove the used candle and place a new one on the pad. You can switch scents anytime.

Lumos Pad FAQ

Q. Do I need the Lumos Pad?
Yes, the Lumos Pad is essential for the auto-ignition feature.
Q. How long does the battery last?
Once fully charged, it lasts over 4 months (based on using 4 times a day).
Q. How do I charge it?
Use a micro USB (5-pin) cable to charge the pad.
Q. Is it waterproof?
No, it is not waterproof. Avoid using it in humid places like bathrooms. Be careful not to let water or liquids enter the pad.
