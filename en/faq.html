FAQ

Q. How long does it take to charge?
A. It takes about 3 hours to fully charge, and lasts over 4 months on a single charge based on daily usage.
Q. Can I use regular candles?
A. We recommend using Lumos dedicated refill candles. They use special wicks optimized for auto-ignition.
Q. What is the warranty period?
A. We provide a 1-year warranty from the date of purchase (excluding damage caused by user negligence).
